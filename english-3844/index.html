Are Food Trends worth it?

This blog is an insight to popular food trends and aims to stir a conversation about what's underneath their surface. The article points towards what the food industry wants to sell us and while discussing the truth about weight loss diets, coffee, vegan meat and fermented foods.

Communication Conundrums: Netflix's Censorship

In this podcasr, my team and I have talked about Netflix and the censorship policies it has to abide by in different countries about the world. We try to focus on different aspects in media and how they are displayed like religion, food,and politics. We also mention how different countries react to disregarded cultural norms in movies and tv shows.
